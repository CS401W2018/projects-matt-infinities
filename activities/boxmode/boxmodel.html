Answers To Questions

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags works as division of parts of HTML, this means that block tags will provide you boundaries and organization for you manipulate and customize aesthetically. You can adjust its margin, border, and padding, this can only be done in block tags. The Inline tags is opposite, you cannot adjust its margin, border and padding because most of its work are intended for texts in HTML.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

All these three components are specific to customizing the spaces and areas the element or block has. Margin is the one responsible for the spacing outside the block, it will help you manage the spaces from one block to another. Border is the one responsible for the space inside the margin and marking as the of the padding, in other words, it is the space between padding and margin. Padding is the one repsonsible for the spaces between padding and the element.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

There are various ways to use as measurement in CSS. Here are some of the common measurement that we can use in CSS. The px meaning pixel is used to measure a pixels in page or image. cm or Centimeters can be use in CSS to measure something in centimeter. pt which is point can also be use to measure something in a manner of point

When would you use a figure tag?

The use of figure tag is highly recommended if you are going to put an image with caption in the website. It will help you control how both the image and caption will look like as one block in the page. On the other hand, using figure tag will also affect how the level of SEO the page has because google or other platforms will indentify the figure as role playing part of the webpage you are creating.
