Coldplay

Coldplay is a British rock band that originated and grouped on 1996. This band is famous to their taste in genre. With their melodic piano, accompanied by harmonuous guitars, and modernize pattern of beat, this made the Coldplay a worldwide mainstream. They started as a local band where they performed in public areas for free. They even have performed on subway stations and have experienced exploring their country just as a local band. But their journey doesn't end there. Their talents have brought them worldwide to lift one and another.

The styles of their song composition and production is unimaginably unique.This made them famous to the world of mainstream. Other than the unique composition and production, they also have an inspiring experiences that made their song move into a whole another level. Every song they've made to the public is exceptional & inspirational to hear. The list below describes the most inspiring songs they've ever made that made my life great in every negativity.

  1. Paradise (12 September 2011)
  2. Yellow (26 June 2000)
  3. Hymn For the Weekend (29 January 2016)
  4. Sky Full of Stars (19 June 2014)
  5. The Scientist (15 April 2003)
  6. Something Just Like This (22 February 2017)
  7. Viva La Vida (25 May 2008)
  8. Fix You (5 September 2005)

This is a quote from their song Viva La Vida (25 May 2008) reminds me of the strength I need during my missionary life and even after serving my mission.
